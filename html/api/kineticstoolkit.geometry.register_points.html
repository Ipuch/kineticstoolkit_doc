kineticstoolkit.geometry.register_points

register_points(global_points, local_points)[source]

Find the homogeneous transforms between two series of point clouds.

Warning

This function, which has been introduced in 0.4, is still experimental and may change signature or behaviour in the future.

Parameters

  • global_points (array of shape Nx4xM) – Destination points as a series of N sets of M points.

  • local_points (array of shape Nx4xM) – Local points as a series of N sets of M points. global_points and local_points must have the same shape.

Returns

Array of shape Nx4x4, expressing a series of 4x4 homogeneous transforms.

Return type

np.ndarray
